General observations

The Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient algorithm was implemented and
compared against the REINFORCE policy gradient method.
One problem which will influence the comparability is that they are running
on different environments, REINFORCE on a discrete one (CartPole)
 and DDGP on a continuously one (Pendulum).

The goal of DDPG is to stabilize learning, which in my opinion can be seen in the plot.
The orange DDPG curve learns smoothly and consistently and is then more stable with smaller
oscillation. The blue REINFORCE curve learns quickly but has a high oscillation.

I think the first part is not totally comparable because of the different environments CartPole vs Pendulum,
but the second part of the plot with the strong vs small oscillation, is quite meaningful.
